- **Usage Data**: Information about your interactions with the app, including:
  - Number of AI requests made
  - Credits consumed and remaining

  - Screen views and navigation patterns
  
- **Device Information**: 
  - Device type and model

  - Mobile network information

- **Analytics Data**: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns and improve user experience

### Information from Third-Party Services

We integrate the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Firebase Services** (Google):

  - Firebase Analytics
  - Cloud Firestore

- **Google AdMob**: For displaying advertisements and rewarded ads
- **In-App Purchase Platform**: Apple App Store or Google Play for subscription management

## Data Storage and Security

### Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Preferences, settings, and cached data are stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Conversation history, user credits, and subscription data are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore
- **Data Location**: Your data may be processed and stored in servers located in various countries where Firebase operates

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication through Firebase
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Secure third-party service integrations

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
